Pulp Fiction

1994

This is a fun tribute page to my favorite movie!

I love how this film tells its story in three separate but intertwining tales. The dialogue is engaging, interesting, and often hilarious.

  1. Laurence Fishburne was meant to play Jules
  2. John Travolta's character is always in the bathroom when something bad happens
  3. Quentin Tarintino plays Jimmy, but was originally meant to play Lance
